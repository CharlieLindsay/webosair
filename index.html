Flight Facts!

Interesting information and history in aviation!

Largest Plane

Currently, the heaviest and longest plane in the world is the Antonov An-225 Mriya. However, only one was ever flown. There is a plane in development (the Radia WindRunner) which will surpass this size, being 24m longer!

Largest Helicopter

The largest helicopter in the world currently is Mil Mi-26 and was built by the Soviet/Russian Mil Design Bureau. It's maximum takeoff weight is 56,000 kg!

Extra facts!
